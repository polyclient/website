---
title: System requirements
---

<Callout>
    PolyClient supports **Linux**, **macOS**, and **Windows** on **x86_64** and **ARM64** architectures.
</Callout>

## Supported platforms

### **Linux**
- **Distributions:** Any with **glibc ≥ 2.31** or **musl ≥ 1.2.2**  
- **Formats:** `tar.gz`, `deb`, `rpm`, `apk`, `pkg.tar.zst`

### **macOS**
- **Versions:** **macOS 11 (Big Sur) or later**  
- **Architectures:** `x86_64`, `aarch64`  
- **Format:** `tar.gz`

### **Windows**
- **Versions:** **Windows 10 (21H2) or later**  
- **Architectures:** `x86_64`, `aarch64`  
- **Format:** `zip`

## Minimum requirements
- **CPU:** `x86_64` or `ARM64`  
- **RAM:** `512 MB – 1 GB` (headless mode, no GUI)  
- **Disk:** `~20 MB` (binary + config + logs)

## Recommended requirements
- **CPU:** `2+ cores` (`x86_64` or `ARM64`)  
- **RAM:** `2 GB+` (for smooth GUI operation, especially with large datasets)  
- **Disk:** `~100 MB` (includes third-party plugins)

## Optional requirements
- **Internet connection:** Required for downloading plugins and updates.  
- **Docker:** Required for running PolyClient in a Docker container.
